Conduct Online Hackathons to build brand awareness among the Tech Community

Branding Challenges

  • Tech Startups often find it difficult to create awareness about their cool new product/ service/ app among the Tech Community
  • One of the challenges faced by startups during the recruitment process is lack of familiarity with the brand. College students as well as experienced developers are often unwilling to attend interviews conducted by startups that they have never heard of.

Conduct Hackathons to build brand awareness

The best way to overcome this problem and improve brand perception is to conduct hackathons and offer prizes to the top coders. This encourages college students as well as experienced techies to attend hackathons and slowly improves brand familiarity. And such hackathons also serve as a great way to recruit top performers!

We encourage companies to conduct hackathons with themes that closely match the sponsor's brand.

Technical Issues

Technical issues faced by the organization could be taken and converted into coding tests (Write an algorithm to identify the top three books to be recommended for a particular reader in an online book store).

Design Skills

Design skills could be tested by asking candidates to design alternative web pages and logos for the company (Design a web page offering valentine's day gift vouchers from an e-Cart Application).

Mini Projects

Mini-projects in the domain of the company could also increase interest among techies (Build a Dashboard to track late deliveries for a Food delivery company).
